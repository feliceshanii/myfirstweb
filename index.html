Ingredients

1. 8 tablespoons of salted butter
2. 1/2 cup white sugar
3. 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
4. 1 teaspoon vanilla
5. 1 egg
6. 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour (6.75 ounces)
7. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
8. 1/4 teaspoon salt
9. 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Step by Step

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
Line three baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
3. Set the bowl aside for now.
4. In a large bowl, use a stand mixer or a handheld mixer to beat the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until they are well combined and creamy.
5. Beat in the eggat a time, followed by the vanilla extract, mixing until everything is fully incorporated.
6. Gradually add the flour mixture into the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until the dough is just combined. Avoid overmixing.
7. Stir the chocolate chips into the dough with a spatula until they are evenly distributed.
8. Roll 2–3 tablespoonfuls of dough into balls and place them on your prepared baking sheets, spaced about 2 inches apart.
9. Place the sheets in the preheated oven and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown. The centers may still look a little soft.
10. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before moving them to a wire rack to cool completely.
